How It Works

Our Lost and Found website helps people report lost items and return found belongings quickly and safely. Please refer to the instructions given below.

1

Report an Item

Submit a report for a lost or found item by filling out the form. Provide details like item name, description, location, date, and an optional photo of the lost item.

2

Connect and Recover

If you find a match, contact us and we will arrange connections to a available lost and found staff.

Safety & Guidelines

Here are the safety & guidelines of TUKLAS, please follow these guidelines when posting or claiming items.

1. Provide Honest Information

When posting a lost or found item, provide accurate details such as the item's color, brand, or unique features to help identify it.

2. Verify Ownership

If you are claiming an item, be ready to describe specific details about the item to prove it belongs to you. Additionally, if you are claiming an item, please contact us, our contact information will be provided in the Contact page

3. Meet in a Safe Place

Arrange to meet within campus grounds, your safety is not guaranteed outside of the Campus

4. Update Item Status

If your item has been found or returned, please update the listing so others know it has been resolved.
