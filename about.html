About TUKLAS

Discovering Innovation, Building Excellence.

Our Mission

TUKLAS is a Lost and Found platform created to help students, faculty members, and staff easily report and locate lost items within the campus. Our goal is to make the process of finding missing belongings faster and more organized

Through this website, users can view reported lost items, post found objects, and connect with the rightful owners. The system is designed to be simple, accessible, and helpful for everyone in the school community.

Innovative

Our system provides a simple digital platform where lost and found items can be reported and tracked easily.

User-Friendly

The website is designed to be easy to use so students and staff can quickly report or search for missing items.

Collaborative

A project built through the combined passion and effort of Group 5.
